## What is it?

FirePath is an AI financial coach that helps you understand the real cost of every spending decision — not in dollars, but in time to financial independence.

"This $5 coffee just delayed your FIRE date by 2 days."

## Why am I building this?

The FIRE (Financial Independence, Retire Early) community has great spreadsheets but terrible software. Most tools are either too simple (basic calculators) or too complex (full accounting systems). Nobody has built an AI-native experience that actually coaches you.

## Core Ideas

- **Impact framing** — every expense is measured in "days to FIRE," not just dollars
- **AI coaching** — personalized insights based on your spending patterns and goals
- **Plain-text accounting** — powered by Beancount, so your data is yours forever
- **FIRE goal simulation** — project your independence date based on real spending
