## What is it?

FirePath is a platform I built to manage my entire financial picture in one place. I'm someone who cares deeply about passive income and financial independence. I need to know that my portfolio is growing, and at what speed — so I can keep my finances under control.

## Why am I building this?

I wanted a single place to bring all my assets together and answer two questions:

1. **How fast is my wealth growing?** — tracking both the investment side (returns, portfolio performance) and the spending side (where money goes, what's eating into growth)
2. **Can I interact with my finances more naturally?** — using AI to make the whole experience simpler and more direct, instead of staring at spreadsheets

## Where are we now?

The platform is live with portfolio tracking, spending analysis, and AI-powered insights. Still iterating on the experience.
